FUNCTIONALITY

The app will be available for iOS and Android devices available on Google Play, the Apple store and linked to from the Vasa web site. The QR code for downloading the app will be printed on the back of the ticket. The app is designed to be as user-friendly as possible by incorporating standard app functionalities, such as swiping and tapping.

Once visitors download the app, they will be able to select categories which will then present the appropriate exhibits to the user. As users experience the various exhibits, they will be able to select either “sink” or “swim” on the exhibit to express what they thought. A smaller survey prompt will follow asking brief questions to expand on their selection.

The app is intended to be expandable in the future to encompass temporary exhibitions, or changes within the museum. The app will be available in English and Swedish. Future language options will be available.
